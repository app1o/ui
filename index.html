How Food Buddy Works

Our platform makes it easy to connect surplus food with people who need it most.

Donors

Restaurants, grocery stores, and individuals can donate surplus food that would otherwise go to waste.

Recipients

Food banks, shelters, and individuals can browse and request available food donations.

Delivery Agents

Volunteer delivery agents help transport food from donors to recipients efficiently.

Administration

Admins oversee the platform, manage users, and ensure smooth operations of the entire system.
